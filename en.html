Booking Guide

How to Book

Your step-by-step guide from booking to your first session.
We're here to make the process as easy as possible.

If this is your first visit, please start by reading through our Terms of Use and Consent Form.
Then submit your preferred session times via the form below,
and your therapist will confirm the schedule by email.
※ Sessions are currently available approx. twice a week.

01
Step One
Review Terms & Consent Form

Before your session, please read through the Terms of Use and Consent Form.
Once you have reviewed and agreed to the terms, you can proceed to the next step.
Both Japanese and English versions are available.

利用規約 (JA) Terms of Use (EN) 同意書 (JA) Consent Form (EN)
02
Step Two
Submit Your Preferred Times
Initial Consultation — ¥ 3,000 (tax incl.) / approx. 30 min

Please submit your 1st, 2nd, and 3rd preferred session times via the form below.
Your therapist will review availability and arrange a schedule that works for you.
Sessions are currently available approximately twice a week.

Submit Preferred Times
03
Step Three
Schedule Confirmation by Email

Your therapist will review your preferred times and contact you by email to confirm the schedule.
Please check your inbox (and spam folder) for the confirmation message.
Feel free to reply to the email with any questions.

📧 Confirmation email: Sent to the email address provided in your form submission.
Please allow 1–2 business days for a response.
04
Step Four
Complete the Intake Form

After booking, please complete the intake form before your session.
The information you provide helps your therapist tailor a more effective session for you.
Please bring the completed form to your session, or send it by email in advance.

ヒアリングシート (JA) Intake Form (EN)
05
Step Five
Your Session Day

Join via Zoom at your scheduled time.
We recommend a quiet, comfortable space and the use of earphones.
Relax and feel free to speak openly — your sessions are a safe, confidential space.

💡 What to prepare
A quiet space  /  Stable Wi-Fi  /  Zoom app (free)  /  Earphones recommended
